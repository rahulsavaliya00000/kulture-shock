Safety Standards — Child Sexual Abuse & Exploitation (CSAE) Policy

Published: September 12, 2025 — Last updated: September 12, 2025

Our commitment

Kulture Shock is committed to protecting children and preventing any form of child sexual abuse or exploitation (CSAE). We strictly prohibit any content, behavior, or material that sexualizes, exploits, or harms minors on our platform.

What we do

  • We do not allow creation, uploading, sharing, or promotion of any content that depicts sexual activities involving minors or exploits children.
  • We remove content that violates this policy immediately upon review and prohibit accounts that participate in such activity.
  • We comply with applicable laws and will report illegal activity to the relevant law enforcement authorities where required.
  • We cooperate with child protection organizations and follow industry best practices for content moderation and reporting.

Reporting

If you find content or behavior that may violate this policy, please contact us immediately at info@getkultureshock.com with the following information (if available):

  • A short description of the issue
  • URL or screenshots showing the content (do not forward illegal media — report it to authorities first)
  • Date, time, and usernames involved

We treat all reports seriously. Urgent or clearly illegal content will be escalated to law enforcement as required by law.

Moderation & enforcement

We use a combination of automated tools and human review to detect and remove policy-violating content. Accounts that violate this policy will be suspended or terminated.

Contact

For questions about this policy or to submit a report: info@getkultureshock.com
