Kulture Shock - Privacy Policy

Last Updated: September 2025
Effective Date: September 2025

1. Information We Collect

When you use Kulture Shock, we may collect the following information:

Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, date of birth, and profile photos you provide during registration.

Social Media Login: If you sign in through Facebook or Google, we receive basic profile information as permitted by those platforms.

Profile Information: Photos, bio, interests, and preferences you add to your dating profile.

Usage Data: How you interact with our app, including matches, messages, and app usage patterns.

Location Data: Your general location to help find potential matches nearby (with your permission).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

• Provide and improve our dating service

• Create and manage your profile

• Connect you with potential matches

• Send you important app notifications

• Ensure safety and prevent misuse

• Provide customer support

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information only in these limited cases:

With Other Users: Profile information you choose to make visible is shown to potential matches.

Service Providers: Trusted third parties who help us operate our app (with strict confidentiality agreements).

Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect safety.

4. Data Security

We implement security measures to protect your information, including encryption and secure servers. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

5. Your Rights and Choices

Access and Update: You can access and update your profile information anytime in the app.

Delete Account: You can delete your account and associated data by contacting us.

Location Settings: You can control location permissions through your device settings.

6. Data Retention

We keep your information as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, we remove your personal information within 30 days, except where required by law.

7. Third-Party Services

Our app may use third-party services like Facebook Login and Google Sign-In. These services have their own privacy policies that govern how they handle your information.

8. Children's Privacy

Kulture Shock is intended for users 18 years and older. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 18.

9. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes through the app or email.

10. International Users

If you're using our service from outside India, please note that your information may be transferred to and processed in India where our servers are located.
