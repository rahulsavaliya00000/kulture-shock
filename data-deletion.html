Kulture Shock - Data Deletion Policy

Last Updated: September 2025
Your Right to Data Deletion

1. Your Right to Delete Your Data

At Kulture Shock, we respect your privacy and your right to control your personal information. You have the right to request deletion of your personal data at any time.

2. How to Request Data Deletion

Option 1: Delete Account from App

• Open Kulture Shock app

• Go to Settings/Profile

• Select "Delete Account"

• Confirm deletion

• Your account and data will be permanently removed within 30 days

Option 2: Email Request

Send an email to: rahul.savaliya@novicbase.com

Subject: Data Deletion Request - Kulture Shock

Include:

• Your registered email address

• Your phone number (if used for registration)

• Reason for deletion (optional)

3. What Data Gets Deleted

When you request data deletion, we will permanently remove:

• Your profile information (name, photos, bio, preferences)

• Your account credentials and login information

• Your chat messages and conversations

• Your match history and interactions

• Your location data and usage analytics

• Any photos or media you uploaded

4. Data We May Retain

For legal and safety reasons, we may retain some information for a limited time:

• Transaction records (if applicable)

• Safety reports and violations (for community protection)

• Data required by law or regulation

• Aggregated, anonymized usage statistics

⚠️ Important Note

Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. You will lose all your matches, conversations, and profile information. Make sure you want to permanently delete your account before proceeding.

5. Deletion Timeline

Immediate: Your profile becomes invisible to other users

Within 7 days: Your data is removed from active systems

Within 30 days: Complete data deletion from all systems and backups

6. Third-Party Data

If you signed up using Facebook or Google, please note:

• Deleting your Kulture Shock account does NOT delete your Facebook/Google account

• You may need to revoke Kulture Shock's access in your Facebook/Google settings separately

• This prevents future data sharing between platforms

7. Verification Process

To protect your account security, we may ask you to verify your identity before processing deletion requests made via email. This may include:

• Confirming details from your account

• Sending verification from your registered email

8. Data Deletion for Inactive Accounts

Accounts that remain inactive for 2 years may be automatically deleted as part of our data retention policy.

9. Partial Data Deletion

If you want to delete specific information rather than your entire account, you can:

• Delete individual photos from your profile

• Clear chat conversations

• Update or remove profile information

These can be done directly in the app settings.

10. Questions or Issues

If you have questions about data deletion or face any issues:

Email: rutu@novicbase.com

Response Time: We aim to respond within 72 hours

App Support: Use the "Help" section in the Kulture Shock app

Compliance: This data deletion policy is designed to comply with privacy regulations including GDPR and other applicable data protection laws. We are committed to protecting your privacy rights.
